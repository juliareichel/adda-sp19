Appetizers

Meatballs

  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs

  • boil on high for 30 min, then switch to low for an hour

Mains

Lucky Chicken

  • bread the cutlets
  • fry them until a little brown
  • saute 2 onions
  • chop 1 red peper
  • sauce: 1 cup of ketchup, 18 oz. jar of apricot jelly, 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • cover chicken and bake on 350 for 45 min

Chicken Poppers

  • chicken cutlets cut in pieces
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp of garlic powder
  • corn flake crumbs
  • sauce: 1/2 cup of sugar, 1/4 cup of honey, 1/2 cup of brown sugar, 1/3 cup of hot sauce
  • fry until light brown
  • bake on 350 for 20 min

Tomato Basil Chicken

  • 2 chicken cutlets cut in eighths
  • 2 large tomatoes cut into chunks
  • 1 8 oz. bottle of wishbone robusto italian dressing
  • 1/2 cup of bresh basil
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 5 sundried tomatoes
  • bake on 350 for 45-60 min

Pretzel Chicken

  • fried version: dip cutlets in egg and then crushed pretzels
  • baked version: dip cutlets in oil and then crushed pretzels
  • bake at 350 for 25 min on one side, then flip and bake 25 min on other side

Pulled Brisket (First Cut)

  • one onion: slice and put on bottom of crock pot
  • 1.5 oz. sweet babt raise bbq sauce: pour some over onion
  • 3 pounds of first cut brisket
  • pour rest of sauce
  • cook on low for 6.5 hours
  • cool for 30 min, then pull brisket

California Roast

  • 4 pound roast
  • 1 onions sliced in rings
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/2 cup of bbq sauce
  • 2 tbsp of brown sugar
  • bake covered on 350 for 2 hours

Turkey Roast

  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of ketchup
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • 3 tbsp of balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tbsp of soy sauce
  • bake covered on 450 for 1 hour, then reduce to 350 for 45-60 min uncovered

Meat Puff-Pastry

  • 2 sheets of puff pastry
  • spice 2 pounds of chopped meat
  • cook the meat in a pan until brown
  • drain the oils
  • put meat back on pan and heat with enough marinara sauce to cover meat
  • place meat in between puff pastry
  • brush puff pastry with egg and water
  • bake at 350 for 1 hour

Strawberry-Apple Kugel

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 3/4 cups of canola oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • mix all together to create crumb topping
  • sprinkle 1/3 of crumb on bottom of 9 by 13 inch
  • peel and cut 3 granny smith apples
  • pour apples and sprinkle with cinnamon
  • sprinkle 1/3 of crumb over apples
  • pour 21 oz. strawberry pie filling
  • sprinkle 1/3 of crumb over filling
  • bake uncovered on 350 for 1 hour
